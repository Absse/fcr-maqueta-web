Who We Are

At FCR Consultants, we lead digital transformation through world-class SAP solutions.

Since 2018, we have supported companies such as Codelco, LATAM, and Carozzi in implementing, supporting, and optimizing their SAP technology.

With over 5 years of experience, we help leading companies achieve digital transformation with a focus on efficiency, innovation, and measurable results.

Our highly specialized team and strategic partnerships, like IBM, foster innovation, transparency, and excellence in every project.

Our Services

We enhance your digital transformation with world-class SAP solutions.

SAP Basis

We install, migrate, and maintain your SAP environment updated with the latest technologies, offering continuous monitoring, patch management, and 24/7 specialized support to ensure optimal and secure performance.

Solution Manager

We implement and configure SAP Solution Manager for centralized management of your SAP environment, including real-time monitoring, project management, process documentation, and specialized technical support.

Security

We strengthen the security of your SAP environment with role design, SAP GRC implementation, and optimized audits, ensuring regulatory compliance and effective risk management.

Strategic Alliance

As IBM partners, FCR Consultants have collaborated for over five years in executing strategic digital transformation projects. We offer tailored SAP solutions, integrating IBM’s best practices and technologies to maximize efficiency, innovation, and results for our clients.

GRC

We analyze business processes, design customized controls, map regulations (GDPR, SOX, HIPAA), and generate compliance reports, ensuring effective control and risk mitigation.

Audit

We design audit strategies tailored to your company, automate data collection and analysis processes, and ensure compliance with best practices and regulatory standards.
